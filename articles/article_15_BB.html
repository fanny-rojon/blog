The Best Banana Bread Recipe

My relationship with Banana Bread started on a gloomy Sunday morning, in my kitchen, staring at 3 very pathetic looking old bananas. Didn’t want to throw them away, had few eggs in the fridge, flour, sugar, butter… Decided to do something with it. And here we are 3 years later, Banana Bread is a staple for me, I bake it for my birthday, for my friends’ birthdays, and any occasion I can think of. Here is the best recipe I’ve encountered. It is from a French book called Les Cakes de Sophie. (highly recommend it!)
