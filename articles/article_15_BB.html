The Best Banana Bread Recipe

My relationship with Banana Bread started on a gloomy Sunday morning, in my kitchen, staring at 3 very pathetic looking old bananas. Didn’t want to throw them away, had few eggs in the fridge, flour, sugar, butter… Decided to do something with it. And here we are 3 years later, Banana Bread is a staple for me, I bake it for my birthday, for my friends’ birthdays, and any occasion I can think of. Here is the best recipe I’ve encountered. It is from a French book called Les Cakes de Sophie.
(highly recommend it!)

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 150g sugar
  • 160g flour
  • 1 bag raising powder
  • 170g butter
  • 3 bananas
  • 100g chocolate
  • Brown sugar

Preparation

  • Preheat the oven to 180°C (Th.6).
  • Put the butter, bananas & brown sugar in a pan and let the bananas caramelize. Set aside.
  • In a bowl, mix the eggs & the sugar.
  • Add the flour & raising powder.
  • Add the melted butter.
  • Add the banana mixture once cool and the chocolate chips.
  • Bake for 45min.
